Fairwheel Bikes

Product Photography
Product photography for an internationally recognized retailer of bespoke bikes and hard to find components.

Fairwheel Bikes is a one of Tucson's premiere bike shops catering to cyclists of all varieties.

Retailers of high-end and exotic bikes and components, Fairwheel has collaborated with artist and designer Geoff McFetridge on several builds, and works with an array of custom framebuilders to provide one of a kind bikes for their clients.
