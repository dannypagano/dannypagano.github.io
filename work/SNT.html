Scintillation Nanotechnologies

Identity Design
Work completed at Godat Design ↗
Identity design for a life sciences startup empowering new, unseen insights in biological research.

Scintillation Nanotechnologies is a University of Arizona-born startup, engineering next generation detection of radiolabelled compounds.

Their proprietary nanoparticles offer unique benefits over tradtional SPA beads and other radioisotope detection technologies.

To describe their various product features we designed a series of unique icons.

The 5x7 grid was inspired by both the sphere shape of the nanoparticles, but also the grid of well-plate wells used in many life science applications.
